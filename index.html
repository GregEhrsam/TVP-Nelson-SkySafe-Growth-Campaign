Project Air Guardian

Motorola & SkySafe Strategic Sales Growth Playbook

Unlocking a Critical Market

The proliferation of unauthorized drones presents a clear and growing threat to public safety and critical infrastructure. The Motorola-SkySafe partnership is uniquely positioned to address this challenge, offering best-in-class detection and mitigation solutions. This playbook outlines five strategic campaigns to capture a significant share of this emerging market.

Campaign 1: The North Dakota Project

Establish a national Center of Excellence with key partners Northern Plains UAS and Grand Sky. This initiative will serve as a live showcase, validating our technology at scale and creating a powerful reference case for statewide deployments.

This phased approach ensures robust integration and culminates in a high-impact demonstration day for nationwide stakeholders, solidifying our leadership in large-area UAS security.

Campaign 2: 2026 FIFA World Cup

The World Cup represents a monumental, time-sensitive opportunity to protect millions of attendees across 11 host cities. This campaign focuses on securing these high-profile venues, establishing a blueprint for major event security worldwide.

Campaign 3: Customer Expansion

Leverage existing SkySafe deployments as beachheads for regional expansion. By demonstrating success with a primary agency, we will execute a "Land and Expand" strategy to sell into adjacent entities like county sheriffs, airports, and stadiums.

Campaign 4: Enterprise Direct

Target critical infrastructure and large enterprises vulnerable to drone-based threats like espionage, disruption, and physical harm. This campaign focuses on protecting assets, data, and personnel for Fortune 500 clients.

The enterprise market is projected to grow significantly, with energy and logistics sectors leading demand. Our solution provides the robust security these high-value industries require.

Campaign 5: DHS Playbook

Establish the Motorola-SkySafe solution as the gold standard for DHS-led National Special Security Events (NSSEs). Moving beyond the World Cup, we will create a repeatable playbook for events like the Super Bowl and the 2028 Los Angeles Olympics.

Our capabilities directly align with core DHS mandates, positioning us as the ideal long-term partner for securing the nation's most critical events and locations against aerial threats.
