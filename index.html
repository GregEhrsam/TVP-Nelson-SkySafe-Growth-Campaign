MOTOROLA & SKYSAFE

A Strategic Sales Playbook for Counter-UAS Dominance

The unauthorized use of drones poses a growing threat to public safety and critical infrastructure. The Counter-UAS market is experiencing explosive growth to address this vulnerability.

$10.5B Projected Global C-UAS Market by 2028

This playbook outlines six strategic initiatives to position the Motorola-SkySafe partnership as the market leader across key sectors, capturing significant market share and driving unprecedented growth.

Target Market Opportunity

Addressable Market Breakdown

Our strategy focuses on four high-potential markets, each with distinct needs and significant revenue potential. The Federal Sector and State/Local Governments represent the largest immediate opportunities due to regulatory mandates and public safety charters.

  • Federal Sector: Critical infrastructure protection, border security, and federal facility safeguarding.
  • State & Local Gov: Law enforcement, emergency response, and protection of public spaces.
  • Venue Operators: Securing stadiums and major events from aerial threats.
  • Large Enterprise: Protecting data centers, industrial sites, and corporate campuses.

Six Strategic Growth Initiatives

1. North Dakota Project

Leverage our partnership with Northern Plains UAS and Grand Sky to establish a statewide drone defense network, creating a blueprint for other states to follow.

Key Focus: State-level Integration

2. 2026 FIFA World Cup

Secure contracts with all 11 U.S. host cities to provide comprehensive airspace security, showcasing our capabilities on a global stage.

Key Focus: Marquee Event Security

3. SkySafe Zones Expansion

Target existing customer locations to cross-sell and upsell services, expanding from single-agency deployments to broader, multi-agency "SkySafe Zones".

Key Focus: Customer Base Penetration

4. Large Enterprise Direct

Identify and engage Fortune 500 companies with critical infrastructure, developing tailored solutions for their specific physical security needs.

Key Focus: New Customer Acquisition

5. DHS Playbook

Develop a comprehensive engagement strategy for the Department of Homeland Security, extending beyond one-time events to long-term security programs like the 2028 Olympics.

Key Focus: Strategic Federal Partnership

6. Federal Sector Expansion

Pursue opportunities within the Departments of Justice, Defense, and Energy, leveraging initial DHS success to penetrate the wider federal market.

Key Focus: Broad Federal Market Capture
